Clara is curious. She seeks to cultivate critical perspectives through her work as a researcher and designer. Trained in architecture with a background in cultural anthropology, her work explores the intersections of urban and social processes through drawing, writing, and animation. She is interested in developing research projects in governmental building design and their use as technologies of governance.

Clara holds a Bachelor of Arts in Cultural Anthropology from UCLA and a Masters of Architecture from Columbia University.

Her skills include creating drawings, animations, and diagrams using the Adobe suite of products, Rhino 3D, CAD, ESRI ArcMap, and 3DS Max.

Her drawings and animations have been published here:

  1. Locations of Justice Series: After Arrest (Urban Omnibus, 2017)
  2. Locations of Justice Series: The People's Court (Urban Omnibus, 2018)
  3. Stalled! Online (Joel Sanders Architects, 2018)
